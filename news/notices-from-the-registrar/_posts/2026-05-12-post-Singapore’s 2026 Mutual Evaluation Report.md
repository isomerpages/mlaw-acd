Dear Regulated Dealer,

The Financial Action Task Force (“**FATF**”) published a peer evaluation report of Singapore, which strongly affirmed that Singapore has a robust and effective framework and process to counter money-laundering (“**ML**”), terrorism financing (“**TF**”) and proliferation financing (“**PF**”).

Regulated dealers may refer to the [press release](https://www.mas.gov.sg/news/media-releases/2026/singapore-has-a-robust-framework-for-combatting-financial-crime-according-to-international-body) jointly published by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (“**MAS**”), the Ministry of Home Affairs (“**MHA**”), and the Ministry of Finance (“**MOF**”).

The [published report](https://www.fatf-gafi.org/content/fatf-gafi/en/publications/Mutualevaluations/mer-singapore-2026.html) is available on the FATF website. The report has also been uploaded at the [ACD website](https://go.gov.sg/minlawacd) in the “Compliance” section, under “AML/CFT/CPF Resources”.

The fight against ML/TF/PF is a shared responsibility. We thank you for your continued support and partnership to keep Singapore’s AML/CFT/CPF regime strong and trusted. 

Anti-Money Laundering/Countering the Financing of Terrorism Division (“**ACD**”)

Ministry of Law
