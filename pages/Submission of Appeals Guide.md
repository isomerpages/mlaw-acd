<p>Registered dealers aggrieved by the Registrar of Regulated Dealer’s (the
“<strong>Registrar</strong>”) decision to impose conditions of registration
under section 9(1) of the Precious Stones and Precious Metals (Prevention
of Money Laundering, Terrorism Financing and Proliferation Financing) Act
2019 (“<strong>PSPM Act</strong>”) can submit an appeal to the Minister
of Law no later than the 14<sup>th</sup> day after the date the registered
dealer is notified of the imposition of the conditions of registration.</p>
<p>Registered dealers can follow the following step-by-step guide:</p>
<p>Step 1: Proceed to <a href="https://acd.mlaw.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://acd.mlaw.gov.sg</a>.</p>
<p>Step 2: Click onto the Regulated Dealers Tab to access E-Services.</p>

<p>Step 3: Click on to the link stated on the directory for Submission of
Appeals.</p>
